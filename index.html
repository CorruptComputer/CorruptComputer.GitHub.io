Space Station 13 is an open source survival game in which you can play as various roles to either help or sabotauge the station, depending on if you are an antagonist for the round.
AsteroidStation was my main focus when I was active in the community, however the map has since been removed.

Warframe Utility

Warframe Utility is an Android app designed to help speed up some of the processes in Warframe, such as notifying you of alerts, and calculating damage done to each enemy depending on your current mod loadout.
This app was origionally designed by Ben Ezard, however I have been added to the team of developers.
